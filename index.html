Child Safety & Anti-Exploitation Policy

Xuni strictly prohibits any content or behavior that exploits or endangers children, including child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Safety Measures Include:

  • 🚫 Zero tolerance for CSAE or grooming behavior
  • 🛡️ Automated and manual moderation of content
  • 📢 Tools for reporting abusive users and content

Any violations will result in immediate account removal and reporting to appropriate authorities.
